Easy Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • Beef: This easy lasagna starts with ground beef (or ground turkey).
  • Spaghetti sauce: Store-bought or homemade.
  • Cheeses: Cottage cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan.
  • Eggs: Bind the cheese mixture and add richness.
  • Seasonings: Dried parsley, salt, black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Water: Â½ cup around the pan edges before baking.

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Mix cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella, eggs, half the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the layers per the recipe.
  4. Bake covered for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and bake 10 minutes more.
