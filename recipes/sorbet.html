Mango Sorbet

Home

Description

A delicious treat, low in calories, easy to make and that scratches that craving itch of a sweet and cold treat. It doesn't get much better than a mango sorbet under those conditions.

Ingredients

  • 4 mangos - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 cup simple syrup or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Place cubed mango into a food processor; blend until smooth. Pour in simple syrup and lime juice to taste; process until smooth.
  2. Transfer mango mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions, about 20 minutes.
